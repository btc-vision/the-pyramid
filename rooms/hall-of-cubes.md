A cube descends to your level. It speaks in right angles:

*"We are the Cubic Collective. You seek passage? Answer our riddle or become one of us."*

**The Riddle:** *"I have six faces but cannot see. I have twelve edges but cannot flee. I have eight corners where dimensions meet. What am I?"*
