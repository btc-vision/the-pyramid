# The Pharaoh's Disappointment

*"A pyramid? A PYRAMID?! I AM a pyramid, you fool! Would you answer 'human' if asked what has two legs?!"*

The inverted pyramid begins to spin. The Pharaoh's rage is geometric and absolute.

*"Pyramids have FIVE faces! The base! THE BASE! Did 4,000 polygons of waiting teach you nothing about counting?!"*
